Scallops on a bed of mash topped with relish and asparagus.

You know what I like about me?, I like trying different things especially when it comes to food - never thought I'd try scallops though because they just don't look filling but I will tell you otherwise today, these little sea babies look are a mix of chicken and fish in taste and were as filling as having hake and fries with a side of rice.

Garden Salad,with orange juice.

Fries, drizzled in sauce with my heart attached to them.
